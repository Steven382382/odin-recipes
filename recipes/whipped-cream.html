Whipped Cream

Desciption

This homemade whipped cream is easy to make with just three ingredients and it holds its shape perfectly. Lightly sweetened and extra fluffy, this whipped cream recipe is the perfect way to top off your favorite treats.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until frothy.
  3. Beat in confectioners' sugar and vanilla, continuing to beat, until soft peaks form.
  4. Enjoy!
